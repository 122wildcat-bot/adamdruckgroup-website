About the Group

Built on York County. Trusted across the region.

The Adam Druck Group is a boutique real estate team at Coldwell Banker Realty, led by a York County native whose family has been building homes and businesses here for four generations.

From historic properties in downtown York to new construction across Dallastown, Dillsburg, and Hanover — and equestrian estates across the Mason–Dixon line — we bring a rare combination of hyper-local knowledge, global marketing resources, and a founder's personal investment in every transaction.

We list. We sell. We negotiate. We advise. And we answer the phone — because your home isn't a transaction to us. It's the foundation of your next chapter.

Where We Work

Deep roots. Wide reach.

From the brick rowhomes of downtown York to the farmland of Dillsburg and the quiet streets of Hanover — we serve buyers and sellers across South Central Pennsylvania and into Maryland and Delaware.

York

The historic core. Row homes, adaptive reuse, and a growing arts district.

Dallastown & Red Lion

Family-friendly suburbs with some of the region's most sought-after schools.

Hanover

Historic charm, new construction, and a short commute to Baltimore.

Dillsburg

Rolling countryside, equestrian estates, and a straight shot to Harrisburg.

Harrisburg
